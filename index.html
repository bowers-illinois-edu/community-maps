Later you will be asked to draw your community, but before that happens you will have an opportunity to learn how to draw on a map. On the map you will see 4 markers. Click on the "Start drawing" button to begin the drawing process. Click on each in turn to draw a shape on the map. Clicking again on the first item ends drawing.

If you make a mistake, you may restart the drawing by clicking the "Try again" button. Then click the "Start drawing" button to begin drawing again. You may also wish to trace the lake more closely by adding additional points to your shape. Feel free to practice several times. When you are done practicing click the "Continue" button.

We now need to know your home address. Please enter it in the box to the right and click "Find my location". If the marker is incorrect, you may drag the marker until it sits over your home.

If this marker is far from your home, try entering your address again and clicking "Find my location." When you have found your home on the map, click "Continue."

Now it is time to draw your community. You may zoom in or out by using the bar at the far left. You can drag the map around. Once you can see your community, click the "Add Community" button to start drawing on the map. Either connect your shape or double click to finalize your community drawing. You may add as many shapes as you wish to describe your community by clicking "Add Community." When you are done drawing your community, click the "Continue" button.
